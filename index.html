About

I am a graduate student in School of Mathematical Sciences,Fudan University. My advisor is Guozhen Wang.
I am mainly interested in computational homotopy theory,with particular emphasis on the study of homotopy groups of spheres in both stable and unstable cases.
I am also interested in using computer to study algebraic topology, such as algorithms for cohomology of Hopf algebroids.
